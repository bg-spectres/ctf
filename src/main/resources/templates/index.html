You might want to keep track of the keys you find. Feel free to write them down, but it's not a competition!

There are 9 keys available on this site (the first was just above you!). Some of them will be more difficult to find than others. Here are some tips to remember:

  • Right click on the web page and click "View Page Source" to the see the source code. Sometimes the developer left things behind!
  • Open the developer console in your browser and view the network requests. Maybe there are weird requests on a page?
  • Watch for cookies on the web pages. Sometimes they're used for authentication.
